Hello, My name is Brian and I am a web developer. I use HTML, CSS, Javascript, ReactJS, and NodeJS.

About

I love language. Solving language barriers is commonplace while living abroad and working in a multinational environment. I started coding while teaching and developing curriculum for the medical English program at the University of Health Sciences in Vientiane, Laos. Lao is not a widespread language, and has a minimal presence on the web. Resources our team needed didn't exist, so I learned to code. Writing code and solving problems is engaging and fulfilling. It was a natural transition from working with verbal language.
